About the Project

Welcome to our book recommendation website! Our goal is to provide you with an enjoyable and personalized book discovery experience.
With our vast collection of books and intelligent recommendation algorithms, we strive to help you find your next favorite read.

Features:

  • Curated book recommendations based on your preferences and reading history.
  • User-friendly search functionality to explore books by title, author, or genre.
  • User reviews and ratings to help you make informed decisions.
  • Save your favorite books to revisit them later.

Technologies Used:

  • HTML, CSS ,Flask ,Python ,Bootstrap

We're dedicated to constantly improving our platform and expanding our book database to provide you with the best reading suggestions.

Thank you for using our book recommendation website. Happy reading!
